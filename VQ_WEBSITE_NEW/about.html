Thank you for visiting.

In Vietnamese, Vinh Quang means vitality and glory. We are a family-owned business that strives to serve the health and cultural needs of the community.

We have a long history. Since we immigrated from Vietnam in 1912, our store has been operating for the last 40 years. We are eager to assist you in finding whatever you may need to boost your health. We speak over 10 languages, including Vietnamese, Mandarin, Cantonese, ChaoZhou, Hakka, and Taiwanese.
